SIN'U-RA

PUREST FORM OF WAR

Battlefield Martial Arts

In Ancient times, Martial Arts were developed for the purpose war, not entertainment or sport competition. Society forgets this because modern warfare, for the most part, is “fought” from a distance with heavy reliance on sophisticated technology and weapons. As a result, hand to hand close combat is not a developmental priority within modern militaries.

Sin’u-Ra is NOT a sport or a self defense system. Sin’u-Ra is a BATTLEFIELD MARTIAL ART. Today, individuals competing in “combat sports” and self defense practitioners are referred to as “martial artists” by the misinformed public. The concept of a Martial Artist has been diluted, destroyed, and replaced. Sin’u-Ra represents a return to the original purpose of Martial Arts.

Hand to hand close combat is the purest form of war. A Sin’u-Ra Specialist is an individual who has mastered the purest form of war so that evil does not win. Becoming a Warrior and a Battlefield Martial Artist requires the body of an individual to become a weapon for the purpose of defeating evil in the rawest form of the modern battlefield.

Martial is defined as, relating to or specific FOR WAR. Martial is NOT defined as, relating to or specific for SPORT. In ancient times, a Battlefield Martial Artist would be required to turn his body into a weapon to defeat evil. In ancient warrior societies, this was considered the highest honor. Unfortunately, this is completely absent from today’s society.

“Combat sport” practitioners are athletes and entertainers who prepare to compete in a specific sport competition with rules and referees. These competitions are in a specific discipline against one opponent in a controlled environment.

These sport competitions have a specific time duration for the purpose of entertainment and financial reward. To add to this, there are obviously no multiple enemy or armed enemy situations. All “combat sport tactics and techniques” are based on these realities. Therefore combat sports have no relevance within the rawest form of the modern battlefield.

In addition, self defense practitioners are representatives of a community that combine combat sports with inadequate weapon disarm tactics and releases. The combination of combat sports, inadequate disarm tactics, and defensive reactions represent an excellent formula if the objective is to get badly hurt or killed in a hand to hand close combat situation against evil.

The purest form of war is hand to hand close combat against evil. The path to becoming a true Battlefield Martial Artist requires the individual’s body to become a weapon for the purpose of defeating evil in the rawest form of the modern battlefield. For this to become a reality, specific components must be perfected. Through specific suffering and sacrifice, Sin’u-Ra is earned.
